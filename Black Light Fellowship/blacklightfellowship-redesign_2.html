About the Founder

Rev. Dr. Walter Arthur McCray

In 1979, Black Light Fellowship, a small African-American publishing and media company, formed to begin "shining as a black light of Christ," by producing Black Christian biblical literature. Since then, it has been our mission to spread His message in that literature with as many people as possible.

"Let your light so shine before men, that they may see your good works, and glorify your Father which is in heaven" — Matthew 5:16

Black Light Fellowship is blessed to be able to give good works a place to shine. We invite you to visit the bookstore and explore a range of theological, historical, and spiritual resources for people of African descent and all humanity.
